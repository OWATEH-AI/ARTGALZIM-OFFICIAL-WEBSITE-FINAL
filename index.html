Our Mission
The Sanctuary

Empowering the Next Generation of African Artists

ARTGALZIM CENTER is a creative haven in the heart of Domboshava, Zimbabwe. We are dedicated to youth empowerment, providing a professional world-class platform where local talents can showcase their cultural heritage and share their unique narratives with the global art community.

Zimbabwean Art Heritage

From the ancient rock paintings of Domboshava Caves to the vibrant canvases of today's urban studios - Zimbabwe's visual culture spans millennia. The granite hills that surround our gallery are themselves a canvas of human expression.

Learn More >

Contemporary African Artists

We champion artists who challenge, provoke, and celebrate. Our roster features painters, sculptors, and multimedia artists whose work speaks to global conversations while remaining rooted in African soil.

Women in African Art

ARTGALZIM CENTER actively platforms women artists - their stories of womanhood, identity, resilience and beauty. Our inaugural vernissage celebrated female creatives from across Zimbabwe.
